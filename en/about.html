O FITNESS CENTRU

FITNESS CENTER is a modernly equipped fitness center, adapted to the most diverse workouts and the most diverse fitness needs of exercisers, as well as the individual condition of each member.

FITNESS CENTER offers a large selection of group trainings, as well as a constantly present, friendly staff, which is always available. Fitness instructors and trainers FITNESS CENTER are professionals with many years of coaching experience, and with specialization in a particular type of exercise.

If you want to continue your fitness habits, whether you are a professional athlete or recreational athlete, enthusiast beginner, in this sports facility you will find the best physical activity for you. We do not allow any kind of discrimination or prejudice. Absolutely everyone is welcome in the FITNESS CENTER.
